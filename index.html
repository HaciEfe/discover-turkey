Istanbul / İstanbul

Istanbul is a city that connects Europe and Asia, rich with thousands of years of history.

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk

Atatürk is the founder of modern Turkey. He made major reforms and modernized the nation.

Ephesus / Efes

Ephesus is an ancient city in the Aegean region, known for its well-preserved ruins.

Ottoman Empire / Osmanlı İmparatorluğu

The Ottoman Empire ruled for over 600 years across three continents. Its legacy shaped much of modern Turkish culture.

Göktürks / Göktürkler

The Göktürks were the first Turkic state to use the name "Türk" and left behind the Orkhon inscriptions.

Seljuks / Selçuklular

The Seljuk Empire was an influential medieval dynasty that contributed to Islamic architecture and Turkish unity.
